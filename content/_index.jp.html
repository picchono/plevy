I am a professor at the Conservatoire National des Arts et Métiers, holder of the Chair of design Jean Prouvé, and member of the Dicen-IDF laboratory (EA 7339).

Researching My main focus is the creation of a Japanese philosophy-inspired design framework to think further the relation between design and appropriation, contributing to Transforming Practices. I am also co-organising the seminar Mediums in design with Annie Gentès, CY École de design.
⌈publications · events · PhDs⌋

Teaching My current courses focus on design research and reflective practices, as well as digital skills for professional transition (for master level and professional certifications). I am also responsible for the Master in design in residence at Mobilier national.

Some other things I am setting up the TransFabric at CNAM. I recently presented the project of the chair during my inaugural lecture was presented this year. I am the coordinator of the international association of the societies of kansei research (KEER).
